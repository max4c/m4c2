## Longevity

Objective: increase healthspan

### Sleep
- blackout curtains for **pitch black sleeping**
- lamps for low light night routine for last hour of the day
- temperature(~68 degrees) the goal slightly cold
- 3-2-1 rule
    - 3 hrs before sleep - no food
    - 2 hrs before sleep - no water
    - 1 hr before sleep - no screens
- journal 15 min before bed to clear thoughts
- pray to clear thoughts
- sun alarm
- brush teeth and skin routine in low lamp lighting
- potential changes I am considering
    - mouth tape? mouth guard?
    - Levoit or Phillips air filters

### Exercise
- push, pull, legs 5x a week (muscle mass and strength)
- yoga 1x a week (flexibility)
- bike to work (zone 2 cardio)
- 4 x 4 min sprints 1x a week (VO2 max)

### Teeth
- 2x a day tooth brushing
- electric toothbrush
- floss
- tongue scraper

### Clothing
- working to reduce all plastic clothing
- aiming to use merino wool and tencel wherever possible
    - ibex, ridge merino, icebreaker, smartwool, etc

### Diet
- cook with stainless steel pots and pans to avoid Teflon
- glass or metal tupperware
- old fashioned oats most mornings
- metal refillable water bottles (I use Owala)
- currently use Mediteranian diet with small modifications
- don't drink soda, alcohol, cofffee, nor smoke (I have strong response to caffeine so I use sparingly)
- monthly 1 day fast for autophagy (I've tried longer fasts but currently don't want to decrease muscle mass)
